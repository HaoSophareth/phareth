Privacy Policy

Last updated May 13, 2026

What we access

Missting connects to your Google Calendar to read your upcoming events. We access event titles, start and end times, and video conference links (such as Google Meet URLs).

How we use it

Your calendar data is used solely to display your meetings in the app, send you reminders, and open join links at the right time. Nothing else.

What we don't do

We do not store your calendar data on any server. Everything is fetched directly from Google's API and lives only on your device. We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party.

Authentication

Missting uses OAuth 2.0 with PKCE to connect your Google account. No passwords are ever stored. You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.
